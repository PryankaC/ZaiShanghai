Shanghai is not shy of restaurants. Whatever you want is around the corner. Finding the gems, however, is another story. Here is a selection of places you must try - tasty food with banging deals.

Perry's With lots of bars jotted around Shanghai, you'll be sure to find a Perry's close to wherever you are but there's no doubt that the best one is in Changping Lu. Be sure to get there early to bag yourself a table and play some dice with some locals. Special offers on every night. Cheap beer always. 601 Nanchang Lu (南昌路601号), Metro: South Shaanxi Road Exit 7
Windows Scoreboard Similar vibe to Perry's but here you can enjoy playing a game of pool, darts or beer pong. Super cheap prices with student discounts available too. Be warned of the hoards of prepubescent kids that flock here on the weekends. Nonetheless, it's entertaining to watch them slowly 1 tequila, 2 tequila, 3 tequila, floor. 2F, 212 Tianyaoqiao Lu（天钥桥路212号2F), Metro: Xuhui
Bar Rouge This is a place you must go to in Shanghai. The views of the Bund are incredible and the music is decent sometimes... Find yourself a promoter to get free entry, and go on ladies night to avoid paying the high price of drinks (free drinks for ladies and men lucky enough to have a lady feed them). 37 Anfu Lu (安福路37号), Metro: Changshu Road
Boxing Car If you're getting a bit bored of drinking Tsingtao all the time, head over to Boxing cat. This brewery has some super tasty craft beers and good food to accompany it. They have a regular happy hour of half price beers until 6pm. The Fuxing Lu branch also has a pool table on the top floor. Great bar for chilling with some friends. Rm 203, 2/F, 169 Jianguo Zhong Lu (建国中路169号2楼203室), Metro: Dapuqiao
