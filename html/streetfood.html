Street Food is something most Westerners are a little be skeptical of. You hear all these horror stories of where the oil comes from and the overall hygeine is obviously not five stars. But it's China!! What do you expect?! You'd be foolish to miss out on the opportunity to be a true local and eat super cheap street food whilst sitting on a chair that was made for 5 year olds. (Warning: you're guaranteed to fall off if you've had a few to drink).

Here are some of the things you have to try:

  • Thin mushrooms
  • Rice cakes
  • Chicken heart
  • Lamb

P.S. If you can handle your spice, make sure to ask for "wei la" but be prepared to bond with your toilet the next day.
