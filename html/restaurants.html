Shanghai is not shy of restaurants. Whatever you want is around the corner. Finding the gems, however, is another story. Here is a selection of places you must try - tasty food with banging deals.

Dumplings Xiao Long Bao (小笼包) is a speciality of Shanghai. Join the locals and try this steamed soup dumpling at Loushi's. Trust me you'll keep coming back for more. Only 10 RMB per portion!! 601 Nanchang Lu (南昌路601号), Metro: South Shaanxi Road Exit 7
Hot Pot Hot pot (火锅）originates from Sichuan province but you can't miss out on this experience if you're in Shanghai. Be prepared to get dirty and put your chopsticks skills to the test. Approx 70 to 150 RMB per person 2F, 212 Tianyaoqiao Lu（天钥桥路212号2F), Metro: Xuhui
Pizza It's hard to find a decent pizza （披萨）in Shanghai but you won't be disappointed with Sofia Pizzaria. Not only do they do great pizza, they also have amazing deals everyday of the week. I'd recommend going on a Saturday for the all you can eat and drink!! Saturday's deal 108 RMB 37 Anfu Lu (安福路37号), Metro: Changshu Road
Indian It's hard to find an Indian restaurant（印度菜）with truly authentic Indian food. Rangoli's has some great dishes available in their all you can eat buffet, which change regularly. All you can eat and drink for just 80 RMB!! Rm 203, 2/F, 169 Jianguo Zhong Lu (建国中路169号2楼203室), Metro: Dapuqiao
