Restaurants

Shanghai is not shy of restaurants. Whatever you're in the mood for, you can find with absolute ease. Finding the gems, however, is another story. Here is a selection of places you must try - tasty food with banging deals.

Dumplings Xiao Long Bao (小笼包) is a speciality of Shanghai. This steamed soup dumpling is mouth wateringly delicious, and once you try them at Loushi's you'll keep coming back for more Only 10 RMB per portion!! 601 Nanchang Lu (南昌路601号), Metro: South Shaanxi Road Exit 7 google map embed
Hot Pot Hot pot originates from Sichuan province but you can't miss out on this experience if you're in Shanghai. Be prepared to get dirty and put your chopsticks skills to the test. Ranges from __RMB to __ RMB per person address of hot pot google map embed
Pizza It's hard to find a decent pizza in Shanghai but you won't be disappointed with Sofia Pizzareia. Not only do they do great pizza, they also have amazing deals everyday of the week. I'd recommend going on a Saturday - all you can eat and drink!! Saturday's deal 108 RMB address google map embed
Indian It's hard to find an Indian restaurant with truly authentic Indian food. Rangoli's has some great dishes available in their all you can eat buffet, which change regularly. All you can eat and drink for just 80 RMB address google map embed
